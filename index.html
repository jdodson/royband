--- layout: default title: Roy is the Greatest Band of All Time! ---

Is a two piece rock band out of Portland Oregon that formed in 1999. Listen to our music to experience the rich legacy of one of the greatest bands of all time.

Albums

Our music is available on Bandcamp under the pay as you like model.

  • The Singles (2007) - A collection of songs recorded at various studio sessions over a 4 year period.
  • Ghetto Rock (2000) -  Featuring such hits as "Take a Rocket to Russia," "Hot Like Mel Gibson" and capping with "Jimi Hendrix Sucked at Guitar" this Roy album exists.
  • Live at Ethos (2001) - Recorded live after Ghetto Rock crashed into the world. Lots of live mistakes forever captured alongside the only available live track "Road Rage (The Latest Craze)."
  • My Cats Breath Smells Like Cat Food (1999) - Our first album burned to CD. This album is a mix of live playing direct to a sound board recording and some of the best Roy tracks from 3 previous tape releases.
