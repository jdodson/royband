Roy

Is a rock band out of Portland Oregon that formed in 1998. Listen to our music to experience the rich legacy of one of the greatest bands of all time.

Albums

Our music is available on Spotify, Apple Music, iTunes, YouTube, YouTube Music, Amazon, Amazon Music and Tidal. We're also on Bandcamp under the pay as you like model.

  • The $ingle$ (2007) - A collection of songs recorded at various studio sessions over a 4 year period.
  • Suburban Rock (2000) -  Featuring such hits as "Take a Rocket to Russia," "Hot Like Mel Gibson" and capping with "We are The Grandaddies of Punk Rock" this Roy album will make you change your socks spontaneously. This was the first Roy album to be properly recorded on reel to reel tape and digitally mastered.
  • My Cats Breath Smells Like Cat Food (1999) - Our first album burned to CD. This album is a collection of Tascam four-track Porta studio songs recorded live and in whatever room we were in at the time. Come for the strange collection of songs, stay for that incredible analog tape hiss.
