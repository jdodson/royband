My Cats Breath Smells Like Cat Food

Our first album burned to CD. This album is a collection of Tascam four-track Porta studio songs recorded live and in whatever room we were in at the time. Come for the strange collection of songs, stay for that incredible analog tape hiss.

Released in 1999
